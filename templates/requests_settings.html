What is SeekAndWatch?

SeekAndWatch (seekandwatch.com) lets your friends and family request movies and TV shows from your Plex server without needing access to Plex or Radarr/Sonarr. Approved items sync to this app and are sent to Radarr/Sonarr.

Why it's safe: Your Plex URL and tokens stay on your machine. The cloud service only relays requests and connection keys; it does not store your media or control your server.
