SeekAndWatch is free and open source. Here's how you can help it thrive.

How this project started

SeekAndWatch started as a simple media scanner to recommend movies and TV shows for my wife. It's since taken on a life of its own (Smart Discovery, Plex Collections, Kometa builder, Radarr/Sonarr integration, and more), and I'm glad it's useful to others too. Thanks for being part of it.

Best way to support: spread the word

The most valuable support is telling others about SeekAndWatch when it fits.

  • Recommend us on Reddit when someone asks for tools like ours (smart discovery, Plex collections, Radarr/Sonarr helpers, etc.).
  • Mention us to friends or in communities when people are looking for "something to recommend movies and TV" or "a way to manage Plex collections."

Word of mouth and honest recommendations mean more than anything else.
