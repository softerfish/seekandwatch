Smart Discovery

How it works:
  • Scans your last 5000 Plex history entries and finds similar recommendations.
  • Set how many titles to pull from history (lower = faster).
  • Future Releases: Only shows movies/shows not yet released.
  • International & Obscure: Includes foreign films and lesser-known titles.
  • Results shuffle each time for fresh recommendations.
  • Lucky Mode: Instantly finds a top-rated match from a random favorite.

Tip: Keep this under 30 for fast results. High numbers (50+) will significantly slow down the scan.
